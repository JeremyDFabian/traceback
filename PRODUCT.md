# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Students using a phone or laptop to turn one photographed notebook page and one
lecture PDF into traceable study material. Their primary task is to verify what
the system detected, connect marked ideas to lecture evidence, and approve useful
flashcards.

## Product Purpose

Traceback preserves student control over AI-assisted study material. Success
means a student can correct notebook analysis before it is used, understand
whether a lecture match is reliable, and review flashcards that retain their
source slide and highlight coordinates.

## Brand Personality

Grounded, calm, and tactile. The product should feel like familiar study
materials enhanced with trustworthy context, not an autonomous AI workspace.

## Anti-references

- Generic dashboard or SaaS styling that replaces the existing notebook and
  stationery visual language.
- AI output presented as certain when the match is weak or absent.
- Decorative UI changes unrelated to the student workflow.
- Fixture or demo data silently substituted for live team integrations.

## Design Principles

- Preserve the student's edits as the source of truth.
- Show evidence and confidence before asking for approval.
- Reuse familiar controls and the existing visual vocabulary.
- Keep failures recoverable without hiding them behind demo data.
- Add only what completes the current study workflow.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Changed product UI targets WCAG 2.2 AA. The flow must support keyboard-only
operation, visible focus, semantic labels, live status and error announcements,
and status communication that does not rely on color alone.
