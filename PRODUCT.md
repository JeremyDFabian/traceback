# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Students scanning handwritten notebook pages to review, connect, and retain concepts.

## Product Purpose

Traceback turns scanned notebook pages into an interactive study surface. Students approve detected concepts, see connections across their own pages, and generate grounded study aids without uploading lecture PDFs.

## Brand Personality

Calm, focused, trustworthy.

## Anti-references

Avoid dense analytics dashboards, generic AI interfaces, decorative complexity, and graph views that feel like specialist data-analysis tools.

## Design Principles

- Scanning is the single clear entry point.
- Students approve extracted concepts before the product uses them.
- Preserve the visual relationship between concepts and their original notebook pages.
- Make connections understandable before making them visually impressive.
- Keep advanced study tools approachable and focused.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Meet WCAG 2.2 AA. Support keyboard navigation, reduced motion, readable contrast, non-color state indicators, and responsive phone use.
